Email Marketing 📅 90 days ✍️ Writing Coach 💼 1:1 Consulting

From 1,200 to 8,500 Engaged Subscribers

A writing coach with valuable content but stagnant growth needed systematic email marketing to build audience and convert readers into clients.

🎯 The Challenge

Despite publishing weekly for two years, Sarah's newsletter growth had plateaued at 1,200 subscribers. Open rates declined to 15%, and she had no clear path from subscriber to paying client. Previous sporadic marketing efforts yielded inconsistent, unpredictable results.

💡 The Strategy

Implemented a comprehensive email growth system focusing on quality over quantity:

  • Redesigned lead magnets to attract serious writers, not freebie seekers
  • Created 7-email welcome sequence building trust and demonstrating expertise
  • Segmented audience by interest (fiction vs. non-fiction, beginner vs. advanced)
  • Implemented re-engagement campaign to revive dormant subscribers
  • Built promotional sequences for coaching services that felt helpful, not salesy

📊 The Outcome

Within 90 days, the email list grew to 8,500 engaged subscribers with 42% average open rates. More importantly, Sarah booked 12 new coaching clients directly from email sequences, generating $25,000 in new revenue—while spending less time on marketing, not more.
